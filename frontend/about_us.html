Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate and substantive information on the ever present applications of LLM Assisted Graders. LLM's in general have brought about a vast changing to the dynamics of many industry at and icredible fast pace. Because of this fast pace of development brought about from LLM's the ethical consequences are often times not seen quick enough as the possible broad impacts of such systems may not be seen until after these systems have embedded themselves in the normalicy of society. The academic world is not immune to this as well. Right now the use of LLM assisted graders is on the rise and we feel it is necesary to provide information to professors, students, and interested citizens alike what the advatages and disadvatages of such systems may be.
